Collision Detection Demo on a Franka Emika Research Robot

Research demo by Sajjad Hekmat

This page presents a video demonstration of my work on collision detection in robotic manipulation using a Franka Emika research robot. The demo illustrates experimental behavior in contact-rich interaction scenarios relevant to safe and responsive robot operation.

This project is aligned with my broader research interests in robot manipulation, learning-based robotics, and human-robot interaction.

Source code is intentionally not shared at this stage because the work is planned for future publication.

You can also watch the video directly on YouTube.
