Overview

This page presents a video demonstration of my work on collision detection in robotic manipulation using a Franka Emika research robot. The demo highlights experimental behavior in contact-rich interaction scenarios relevant to safe and responsive robot operation.

The work is aligned with my broader research interests in robot manipulation, learning-based robotics, and human-robot interaction. It reflects hands-on experimentation with robotic systems in settings where robust contact awareness and safe physical interaction are important.

Note: This repository is intended to share a research demo for academic and professional presentation purposes. Source code is intentionally not included because the work is being reserved for future publication.
